Validating Your First Market

schedule 8 min read • Lesson 3: The First Sh1,000

Validation is the process of testing your business idea with real potential customers before you invest significant time or money. In the Kenyan market, this often means moving from a WhatsApp status to a direct transaction.

hub

Illustration: The Feedback Loop between Seller and Buyer

Many entrepreneurs fail because they build a product in isolation. By seeking Sh1,000 from one customer, you prove that your solution has immediate value. This is the "Abundance Mindset" in action—seeing the wealth already circulating in the market.

"If you are not embarrassed by the first version of your product, you’ve launched too late."

— Reid Hoffman
