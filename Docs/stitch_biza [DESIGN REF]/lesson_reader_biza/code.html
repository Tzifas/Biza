Lesson 3: Finding Suppliers

DROPSHIPPING MODULE

Not all suppliers are partners.

Finding the right supplier is the difference between a thriving business and a customer service nightmare. In Kenya, your reputation spreads fast through WhatsApp groups and social media.

When browsing Alibaba, don't just look for the lowest price. Look for "Gold Supplier" status and at least 3 years of trade history. Check their response rate—if they take 48 hours to answer a simple question now, they'll take a week when your shipment is delayed.

For local fulfillment via Jumia or local warehouses, proximity is key. A supplier in Eastleigh might be cheaper, but a supplier with a professional warehouse system will ensure your "Same Day Delivery" promise actually stays true.
