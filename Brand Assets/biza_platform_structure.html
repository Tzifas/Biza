Course roadmap layout — per topic

Every course follows this exact 7-step structure. No fluff. Users always know where they are.
1

What is this? (The real explanation)

Plain-language breakdown of how the income model actually works. No hype. What it is, who does it, why it works.

2

Requirements & reality check

Device needed, internet speed, time per week, skills required, capital needed (honest range), location limitations.

3

How the money actually flows

Step-by-step diagram of how value moves from platform → you → payment. This is what prevents fraud — users see the full chain.

4

The process (action roadmap)

Numbered steps from zero to first income. No skipped steps. Realistic timeline included (e.g. "expect 3–6 months before consistent income").

5

Go-to platforms & tools

Exact platforms listed with direct links. Payment methods that work in Kenya. What to avoid and why.

6

Video resources Admin adds here

Curated YouTube videos you personally vet and embed. Each video gets a one-line note on what to take from it. Admin panel lets you add/remove/reorder videos per course.

7

Red flags & scam warnings

Common scams in this niche. How to spot fake versions. What legit platforms never ask for. This section is non-negotiable on every course.
