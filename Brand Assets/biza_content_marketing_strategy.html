Content strategy — core philosophy

One idea → four pieces of content
You don't create different content for each platform. You create one core idea per week and repurpose it intelligently across all four. This keeps you consistent without burning out.

Script structure — every video/reel

Same formula every time. Claude writes these for you — paste the topic, get a full script.
1

Hook (0–3 sec)

One sentence that stops the scroll. A bold claim, a surprising number, or a direct challenge. E.g. "Most Kenyans trying dropshipping lose money in the first month — here's why."

2

Problem (3–15 sec)

Agitate the pain point. Why people struggle, what misinformation is out there, what scams look like. Make them feel seen.

3

Authority (15–25 sec)

Why Biza breaks it down differently. No fluff, just the real process. Brief and confident — not a CV recitation.

4

Value delivery (25 sec – end of short / 2–10 min for YouTube)

The actual information. Delivered clearly, in steps. Visual aids for YouTube. Fast cuts and text overlays for TikTok/Reels.

5

CTA (last 5–10 sec)

One clear action only. "Full roadmap is on Biza — link in bio." Never ask for likes AND follows AND comments — pick one per video.

Tools & AI workflow

Claude (here)
Script writing, hooks, captions, course content
Write full video scripts, generate 10 hook variations per topic, write Instagram captions, draft course roadmap text, create scam warning copy. Your primary content brain.
Canva
Thumbnails, carousels, brand graphics
Create YouTube thumbnails, Instagram carousel slides, Facebook post graphics. Use Biza brand colors (Forest #1A3A2A + Gold #F5C842). Canva's Magic Write can assist with captions too.
CapCut
Short-form video editing + captions
Auto-captions for TikTok and Reels. Templates for fast edits. Free and powerful enough for everything you need at this stage. Available on mobile.
Buffer / Later
Content scheduling across all platforms
Schedule posts for TikTok, Instagram, Facebook in one place. Plan the whole week on Sunday. Buffer has a free tier that handles 3 platforms.
TubeBuddy / VidIQ
YouTube SEO & keyword research
Find what Kenyans are already searching on YouTube. Optimize titles, tags, descriptions. Know before you film whether a topic has search demand.
Notion
Content calendar + idea bank
Keep all your ideas, scripts, publishing dates, and performance notes in one workspace. Set up a simple content tracker: Topic → Script status → Published → Performance.

Growth funnel — how content converts to revenue

1

Discovery (TikTok / Instagram Reels)

Short, punchy content brings in cold audience. Goal: get them to follow and want more. No hard sell here.

2

Trust building (YouTube / Facebook)

Long-form content converts followers into believers. They see you know what you're talking about. They start visiting Biza.

3

Conversion (Biza platform)

Free course gets them on the platform. They see the depth of the paid roadmaps. KES 250 feels like nothing compared to the value visible.

4

Retention (community + email)

Pro Pass members stay through community and new content drops. Email list (built via free course signup) keeps them engaged month to month.

5

Referral (word of mouth)

Someone who earns their first KES 5,000 online using a Biza roadmap tells five people. This is the most powerful marketing — build the product well first.
