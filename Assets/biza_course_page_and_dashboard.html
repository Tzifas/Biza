How the money flows In progress

You promote a product using a unique link. When someone buys through your link, the company pays you a commission — usually 5–40% of the sale price. You never handle the product or the customer.

How the chain works:

You (content) → Audience clicks your link → Buys product → Company tracks via cookie → Commission sent to your account → Withdraw to M-Pesa or bank.
